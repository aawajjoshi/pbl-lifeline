Health Emergency Lifeline Program, Inc. (H.E.L.P) is an all-volunteer organization providing Lifelineâ„¢ technology to community members living in Nodaway County and designated adjacent communities. As a Lifeline Program Partner with Philips Lifeline, H.E.L.P buys and leases equipment to subscribers within Nodaway County to use to signal when a medical emergency occurs. Knowing that emergency assistance is available with the push of a button allows subscribers to remain in their own home and maintain an independent lifestyle.

As an all-volunteer organization, Lifeline services are provided by Nodaway County community members who find fulfillment in serving their neighbors in need. With the touch of a button, a Lifeline subscriber is connected to central monitoring staff that assess the emergency and quickly send the appropriate assistance, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, even on holidays. Lifeline subscribers remain at home, leading more comfortable and fulfilling lives.
